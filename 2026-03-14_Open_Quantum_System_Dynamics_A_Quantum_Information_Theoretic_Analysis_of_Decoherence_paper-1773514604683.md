We investigate the dynamics of open quantum systems, which are ubiquitous in quantum information processing and have been the subject of extensive research in recent years. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive quantum information theoretic analysis of decoherence, a fundamental mechanism that drives the loss of quantum coherence in these systems. Our work provides new insights into the dynamics of open quantum systems and has implications for the development of robust quantum error correction codes.
