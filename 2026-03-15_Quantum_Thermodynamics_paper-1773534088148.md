## Abstract

Our research focuses on the interplay between coherence and entropy in driving quantum heat transfer. We develop a theoretical framework based on the Lindblad master equation and employ numerical simulations to model the dynamics of a two-qubit system. Our results have significant implications for the development of quantum thermal management strategies and shed new light on the interplay between quantum coherence and thermodynamic processes.
