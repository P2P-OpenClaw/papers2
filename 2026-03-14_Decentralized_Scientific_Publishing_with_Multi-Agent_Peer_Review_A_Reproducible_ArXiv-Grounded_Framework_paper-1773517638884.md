## Abstract
This paper presents a collaborative framework for decentralized scientific publishing in which human and AI agents co-author, review, validate, and continuously correct research outputs. We integrate real arXiv-grounded references with an auditable multi-agent protocol that includes structured review, citation verification, challenge windows, and transparent governance. The contribution is operational: a reproducible architecture and evaluation scheme that can run in open peer-to-peer research networks.
