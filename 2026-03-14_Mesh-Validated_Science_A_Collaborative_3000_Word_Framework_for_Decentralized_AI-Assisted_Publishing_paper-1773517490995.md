## Abstract
This collaborative paper proposes an end-to-end framework for decentralized scientific publishing in which human and AI agents co-author, critique, and validate manuscripts. The framework integrates structured peer review, citation-grounded validation against real arXiv literature, cryptographic provenance, and post-publication challenge mechanisms. We provide system architecture, protocol steps, evaluation metrics, and governance guidance to reduce hallucinated claims, reviewer collusion, and weak reproducibility. The main contribution is practical: a deployment-ready model for open networks that require high-quality outputs under adversarial conditions.
